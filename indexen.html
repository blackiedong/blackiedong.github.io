2023 FIRST International Conference

Tianjin|China



Theme： Interdisciplinarity: The Fusion of Technologies (Semiconductor, Artificial Intelligence, Internet-of-Things, and Communications)


Main Organiser： Tianjin University


Date:30 Oct 2023 (Mon) to 31 Oct 2023 (Tue)


Venue： Student Center, Tianjin University, No. 92 Weijin Road, Nankai District, Tianjin Zip Code: 300072






     Conference Organizer



Tianjin University

It is one of the first batch of national key universities directly under the Ministry of Education of the People's Republic of China, one of the national "double first-class" universities, one of the national "Project 211" and "Project 985" universities, one of the Chinese Academy of Engineering and one of the 10 pilot universities of the Ministry of Education in engineering education reform, and one of the first batch of independent examination units of degree authorization. It has been selected into the national "Strong Base Plan", "2011 Plan", "111 Plan", "Excellent Engineer Education and Training Plan", National innovative Experimental Plan for College students, National Innovation and Entrepreneurship Training Plan for College students, National public graduate Project for Building high-level universities, new engineering research and practice Project, the first batch of scientific and technological achievements transformation and technology transfer bases of higher institutions, and the country College students' cultural Quality Education base, one of the first national demonstration universities to deepen the reform of innovation and entrepreneurship education, one of the eight old building universities, founding member of China-ASEAN Engineering University Alliance, China and Central European Countries Science and Technology Innovation University Alliance.


School of Microelectronics

The School of Microelectronics, Tianjin University, is one of the 26 national exemplary Schools of Microelectronics. It was founded in September 2015 based on School of Electronic and Information Engineering. On 11th November 2016, it became an independent school with 52 staff, including 14 professors, 16 associate professors and 7 lecturers. 48 staff are master tutors, and 28 staff are doctor tutor. There more than 800 undergraduate and graduate students. The school’s atmosphere is rigorous and alive. We cultivate students' international vision and cross-cultural communication skills by bilingual education, 3+1 years international cooperate educating and joint school with Georgia Institute of Technology. Our students have performed well in various technology, culture and sports competitions such as Champion of international microwave conference student design contest modelling project; Special prize of Tianjin innovation and entrepreneurship scholarship; The special prize of “Bao Gang” scholarship; National Champion and international third place of the China-UK business plan competition; and First prize of China university bio-networking entrepreneurship competition.

Every year, over 55% graduates get offer to study on in both national and international reputable university. The employment rate of graduates is about 98%. Most graduates have job offers from leading companies such as China aerospace science and industry group, China aerospace science and technology group, China electronics technology group strategic key units, China mobile, China unicom, ZTE, Huawei, Purple light show sharp, HiSilicon semiconductors, Grace wisdom pu, Unigroup, NXP Semiconductor, SMIC, Beijing Orient science and technology, etc.

The school has a strong discipline. Relying on the first-level disciplines of electronic science and technology, the construction of electronic science and technology doctoral programs and post-doctoral research stations, microelectronics, and solid electronics national secondary key disciplines.

The school’s major consists of Electronic science and technology, Integrated circuit design and Integration system. Graduate training includes microelectronics and solid electronics, circuits and systems, electromagnetic fields and microwave technology, integrated circuit engineering. There are also practical and scientific research institutions such as National IC talent training base, the ministry of education key laboratory of advanced ceramics and machining technology, imaging and perception (one direction), Tianjin microelectronics technology key laboratory, Tianjin integrated circuit design and technology training center, Tianjin IC design center, Integrated circuits and computing systems technology engineering center of Tianjin, Tianjin infrared imaging technology engineering center (with Tianjin shipping technical institute of physics), and Institute of electronic materials and components.

At present, the school is conforming to the national integrated circuit industry development strategy and the requirements of "new engineering" construction, focusing on the fundamental task of cultivating virtue and people, aiming at the goal of building a world-class discipline and a first-class national exemplary microelectronics college, vigorously promoting the construction and development of the school's education and teaching, scientific research, discipline construction and other undertakings, and closely strengthening industry-university-research cooperation with well-known enterprises at home and abroad.



Conference Objective



This international conference aims to discuss the open problems and present new solutions that address the challenges of future communication systems, artificial intelligence, internet-of-things, and chip design. Specifically, the role of semiconductors in future communications will be presented and how can the semiconductor and communication industry emerge stronger after the pandemic will be discussed.
