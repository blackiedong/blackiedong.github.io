2023 FIRST International Conference

Tianjin|China



Theme： Interdisciplinarity: The Fusion of Technologies (Semiconductor, Artificial Intelligence, Internet-of-Things, and Communications)


Main Organiser： Tianjin University


Date:30 Oct 2023 (Mon) to 31 Oct 2023 (Tue)


Venue： Student Center, Tianjin University, No. 92 Weijin Road, Nankai District, Tianjin Zip Code: 300072






     Conference Organizer



Tianjin University

It is one of the first batch of national key universities directly under the Ministry of Education of the People's Republic of China, the first modern university in China and and one of the first key universities under the "Project 211" and "Project 985".

Website: http://www.tju.edu.cn/


School of Microelectronics

It was established in September 2015 relying on the former College of Electronic and Information Engineering of Tianjin University. It is one of the 26 national demonstration microelectronics colleges to build and prepare for construction.

Website: http://sme.tju.edu.cn/


Conference Objective



This international conference aims to discuss the open problems and present new solutions that address the challenges of future communication systems, artificial intelligence, internet-of-things, and chip design. Specifically, the role of semiconductors in future communications will be presented and how can the semiconductor and communication industry emerge stronger after the pandemic will be discussed.
