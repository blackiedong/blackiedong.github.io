2023 FIRST INTERNATIONAL CONFERENCE


Theme：INTERDISCIPLINARITY
     The Convergence of Technologies (Future Communications and Chip Design)


Oct 2023   TIANJIN UNIVERSITY

The theme of the first FIRST International Conference is INTERDISCIPLINARITY: The Convergence of Technologies (Future Communications and Chip Design) Interdisciplinarity draws knowledge from different fields and creates new areas by working across boundaries. Product development is becoming exceedingly complex and innovative products require unrelated technologies combined in new ways. The semiconductor industry has long been a leader in connecting things in the factory while the communication industry has always been a front-runner in connecting people in our daily lives. They are at the heart of technology innovation.

This FIRST international conference will bring together scholars and experts from home and abroad who have made outstanding contributions in the semiconductor field, aiming to discuss open issues and propose new solutions to solve the challenges of future communication systems and chip design. Specifically, it will introduce the role of semiconductors in the future of communications and discuss how the semiconductor and communications industries can become stronger after the COVID-19 pandemic.
