The products operated by the company are mainly used in semiconductor and photoelectric industries, widely used in integrated circuit design, verification, packaging and testing, especially precision device testing and other laboratory products, to ensure the quality and reliability of laboratory products, reduce research and development time and cost.
The company's business purpose is "focus on demand", "provide service", "realize value", "create a better future".

Bring services to customers and the wider external community, achieve win-win results and achieve outstanding brand. Facing the vast market and increasingly fierce market competition in the future, the company will uphold the values of "people-oriented, innovation and sharing", establish long-term cooperative partnership with customers with professional services, and jointly witness the take-off and development of China's science and technology.

Gold Sponsorship



Beijing Dahua Radio Instrument Co. Ltd.
Beijing Dahua Radio Instrument Co. Ltd. (formerly state-owned 768th Factory) was founded in 1958 and is one of the earliest large-scale backbone enterprises of microwave measurement instruments in China. It is now under the jurisdiction of Beijing Electronics Holding Co., Ltd. Dahua has a first-class research and development team in China, with technology reaching international advanced levels. The enterprise has passed ISO and GJB quality system certification, and has been recognized by national laboratories and defense technology industry laboratories. It has also led or participated in the preparation of multiple national and industry standards.
Dahua focuses on the instrumentation industry and provides high-quality and reliable industry solutions. At present, Dahua Electronics' products have covered programmable AC/DC power supplies, electronic loads, automation testing systems, industrial automation instruments such as flow, level, pressure, and safety detection instruments such as combustible and toxic gas detection alarms. They have been widely used in fields such as aerospace, scientific research institutes, university education, 5G communication, industrial control, automotive electronics, new energy, smart water, municipal chemical, metallurgy, and so on.

Website: http://www.dhelec.com.cn/



Wintech Nano (Suzhou) Co. Ltd
Wintech Nano is an internationally leading third-party commercial laboratory in the field of semiconductor chip analysis and testing, as well as a testing center that has mastered the advanced process failure analysis line earlier in China. The company's business focuses on the field of electronics and semiconductor, providing one-stop material analysis (MA), failure analysis (FA), reliability analysis (RA), destructive physical analysis (DPA), vehicle gauge level chip testing and auxiliary research and development TD services, known as the "chip hospital" in the semiconductor industry chain.
Wintech Nano was founded in Singapore in 2004 and came to Suzhou in 2012 as part of China-Singapore cooperation. At present, there are more than 500 team laboratories in Suzhou, Nanjing, Fujian, Singapore and Malaysia. Wintech Nano has CMA/CNAS Laboratory certification, It has been awarded as high-tech Enterprise, Jiangsu Province Specialized and special small and medium-sized Enterprise, Jiangsu Province service-oriented Manufacturing Demonstration Platform, Jiangsu Province strategic emerging Industry and Service industry standardization pilot enterprise, Jiangsu Province R&D Enterprise, Jiangsu Province Semiconductor Chip Analysis and Testing Engineering Technology Research Center, Jiangsu Province Small and medium-sized Enterprise Public Service Platform, Suzhou Leading Producer Service Enterprise, and Suzhou Gazelle Enterprise Industry and unicorn breeding enterprises and other honorary titles.

Website: https://www.wintech-nano.com/

World Scientific Publishing Company
World Scientific Publishing Company is the largest English science and technology publishing company in the Asia-Pacific region and one of the major science and technology publishing companies in the world. Headquartered in Singapore, the company has branches in New Jersey, London, Munich, Chennai, India, Tokyo, Israel and Beijing, Shanghai, Hong Kong and Taiwan. World Science and Technology Publishing Company specializes in publishing high-tech books and periodicals. It publishes more than 600 new books and 130 academic journals every year, among which 62 journals are included by SCI. Since its official launch in October 2006, World Scientific has published more than 6,600 high quality e-books and is on track to add at least 300 e-books with 100,000 pages of content per year. Ebook resources cover a wide range of natural and social hotspots, including: mathematics, physics/nonlinear science, chemistry/materials science/nanotechnology, computer science, engineering, environmental science, Medicine & Healthcare, life science, business and financial economics, popular science, social science and Asian studies.

International Electronics Manufacturing Initiative (iNEMI)
The International Electronics Manufacturing Initiative (iNEMI) is a not-for-profit, highly efficient R&D consortium of 86 leading electronics manufacturers, suppliers, associations, government agencies and universities.
iNEMI roadmaps the future technology requirements of the global electronics manufacturing industry, identifies and prioritizes technology and infrastructure gaps, and helps eliminate those gaps through timely, high-impact collaborations among our members. These collaborations support our members' businesses and help drive the industry by accelerating the deployment of new technologies, developing industry infrastructure, stimulating standards development, and disseminating efficient business practices.
Website: https://www.inemi.org/
