Diamond Sponsorship



XIN RUI Technology Co. LTD
Tianjin XINRUI Technology Co., Ltd. is a service provider focusing on global semiconductor testing technology, promoting the rapid growth of semiconductor industry in China, and providing electrical, optical, magnetic measuring instruments, system integration and technical support for semiconductor research and manufacturing.
The products operated by the company are mainly used in semiconductor and photoelectric industries, widely used in integrated circuit design, verification, packaging and testing, especially precision device testing and other laboratory products, to ensure the quality and reliability of laboratory products, reduce research and development time and cost.
The company's business purpose is "focus on demand", "provide service", "realize value", "create a better future".
The company's business principle is a high sense of responsibility, quality service and leading solutions to help customers truly solve the test problems, and to establish and maintain long-term cooperative relations with customers. Bring services to customers and the wider external community, achieve win-win results and achieve outstanding brand. Facing the vast market and increasingly fierce market competition in the future, the company will uphold the values of "people-oriented, innovation and sharing", establish long-term cooperative partnership with customers with professional services, and jointly witness the take-off and development of China's science and technology.

Website: http://www.tjxinruitech.com/

Platinum Sponsorship



Realtek Semiconductor Corp.
Realtek Semiconductor Corp., located in the Hsinchu Science-based Industrial Park — Taiwan's "Silicon Valley" — began with a small group of young, devoted engineers in 1987. Over three decades, these young founding engineers built Realtek into one of the world's largest and most successful IC design houses. Realtek's efforts to provide the ultimate in pioneering IC technology — along with its firm commitment to creating unique and innovative designs for a broad range of high-tech applications — have won the company a worldwide reputation and made possible a favorable and consistent growth rate in the years since its establishment. We attribute this achievement to Realtek's tradition of excellence.

Premium Sponsorship



World Scientific Publishing Company
World Scientific Publishing Company is the largest English science and technology publishing company in the Asia-Pacific region and one of the major science and technology publishing companies in the world. Headquartered in Singapore, the company has branches in New Jersey, London, Munich, Chennai, India, Tokyo, Israel and Beijing, Shanghai, Hong Kong and Taiwan. World Science and Technology Publishing Company specializes in publishing high-tech books and periodicals. It publishes more than 600 new books and 130 academic journals every year, among which 62 journals are included by SCI. Since its official launch in October 2006, World Scientific has published more than 6,600 high quality e-books and is on track to add at least 300 e-books with 100,000 pages of content per year. Ebook resources cover a wide range of natural and social hotspots, including: mathematics, physics/nonlinear science, chemistry/materials science/nanotechnology, computer science, engineering, environmental science, Medicine & Healthcare, life science, business and financial economics, popular science, social science and Asian studies.

Website: https://www.worldscientific.com/
