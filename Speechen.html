     Main Information about Conference Leader



Professor Kiat Seng YEO
IEEE Fellow, Singapore Academy of Engineering Fellow, Singapore National Academy of Science Fellow, AAIA Fellow. He was previously Associate Provost for Research and International Relations and Chairman of the University Research Board (URB) at the Singapore University of Technology and Design (SUTD). He is currently advisor (Global Partnerships) at SUTD, and distinguished professor at the School of Microelectronics, Tianjin University. Additionally, he is the Chairman of the interdisciplinary subject of integrated circuit science and engineering.
Primary Research Field：Device Characterization and Modelling, Low-power Circuits and Architecture, RF/mm-wave Integrated Circuit Design, Next-generation Wireless Communications.
